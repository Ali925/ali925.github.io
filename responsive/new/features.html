Engage your talents with smart & cloud based questionnaires and quizzes — turn collected data into valuable hires

It’s super easy really: uncover the best applicants by testing their skills and then verify the results to trust your findings. With customizable screening questionnaires, personalized online quizes, automated test scores and a smart rating system, Glorri shines a light on the best talent for all of your job listings.

Improve Candidate Quality through Automated Pre-employment Skill Testing

  • Simply copy & paste your questions to template or create quizzes or pre-screening forms from scratch, categorize your questionsby industry

  • Use wide range of question types: single & multiple choice text questions, short & long answer, picture questions etc.

  • Choose language for your tests, set privacy rules

  • Attach images and videos; include welcoming & closing messages, declare instructions.

  • Define time limits and score per question

  • Select candidates and invite them to take tests remotely before inviting them to interview.

  • View passed and failed results in smart table, get automated scores

  • Share scores with your team, discuss & rate applicants collaboratively

  • Notify candidates by sending them pass or fail messages
