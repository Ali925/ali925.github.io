Frequently asked questions

What is Glorri and how can it help with my recruiting process?

Glorri is at the heart of recruiting — the only candidate-centric applicant tracking system that helps you find, attract and manage top talent in one place. Integrated with recruitment best practices, Glorri takes the hassles out of recruiting and drives decision-making through a centralized and streamlined platform you can access from anywhere.

From building standout brand profiles to creating and promoting customized job postings, evaluating and interviewing applicants, and managing the candidate pipeline, Glorri puts recruiters in control every step of the way.

How much does Glorri cost?

To allow us to deploy new features faster and improve the quality of our service, we're making Glorri available to you free of charge. Sign up for an account and get access to all Premium features for free. All we ask of you is to send us your valuable feedback so that we can continue to make Glorri better together.

We believe in offering a transparent service to our clients, which is why we won't charge you any upfront, setup or cancellation fees. You pay only the monthly subscription you've signed up for, and you're free to cancel it at any time during the billing cycle.

Does Glorri require any training?

At Glorri, we're all about delivering top-of-the-class service that is unparalleled in the industry. Once you sign up for an account, you get access to detailed video explanations covering all features so that your HR team can start recruiting in no time. We also have a friendly online customer support team ready to answer all your questions and assist you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Training services are available and can be provided at your business premises free of charge.

What are the system requirements?

Glorri is fully compatible with the following web browsers:

  • Chrome (versions x and above)
  • Firefox (versions x and above)
  • Safari 10 and later
How many jobs can I post at one time?

With Glorri, there's no limit to the number of job postings you can have at one time. Whether you're recruiting for one position or twenty, you can post as many or as few job offerings as you want.

Does Glorri offer a job portal?

Glorri provides an intuitive and comprehensive job portal that lets you publish job offerings on:

international job boards: source top talent from popular job boards using Glorri's intuitive dashboard;

our built-in job board: once your job posting goes live, it becomes accessible to our integrated database of candidates. Take advantage of our comprehensive filtering system, which lets you filter candidates by very specific criteria — for example, you can view only candidates who have a LinkedIn profile.
